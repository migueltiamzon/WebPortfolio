Description

A school project that required us to design a website for any local business of our choice was Boado's Kitchen's website. We chose this business since it is located in the city of San Fernando and my group and I are both interested in food enterprises.

Boado's Kitchen started as a little side job that eventually turned out to grow into a food catering business. As time went by, little by little, more people knew the business up until it gradually grew into serving a lot of clients. A catering business that makes tasty dishes and also comfort food that Filipinos love.
